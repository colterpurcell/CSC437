How Different Campers Experience This Trip

Family Campers

  • Stay at Bridge Bay or Madison Campgrounds
  • Short boardwalk walks in geyser basins
  • Visitor center exhibits and ranger programs
  • Wildlife drives at dusk in Hayden/Lamar Valleys

Backpackers

  • Backcountry permits for multi-night routes
  • Thermal feature overlooks from adjacent trails
  • Remote lake camps and alpine meadows
  • Early-light photography away from crowds

Bikepackers

  • Grand Loop segments with scenic pullouts
  • Cycling-friendly camp loops where allowed
  • Quiet side roads and overlooks
  • Plan around traffic and shoulder widths

Yellowstone Backcountry Site 4B1

Site Details

Location: Shoshone Lake backcountry area

Designated wilderness campsite for backpackers

  • Site designation: 4B1 (backcountry permit site)
  • Capacity: 8 people maximum
  • Elevation: 7,795 feet
  • Season: June 1 - October 31

Access

  • Path access: Shoshone Lake Trail
  • Path starts from: DeLacy Creek Trailhead
  • Hiking distance: 3 miles from trailhead
  • Difficulty: Easy hike, moderate with full pack

Site Features

  • Setting: Lakeside with Shoshone Lake access
  • Water source: Lake water (requires treatment)
  • Fire restrictions: Designated fire rings only
  • Food storage: Bear boxes provided
  • Privacy: Secluded wilderness location

Camper Types

  • Backpackers: Primary users - lightweight camping gear required
  • Bikepackers: Trail not suitable for bikes - hiking access only
  • Family Campers: Not recommended - wilderness site requires experience

Permits & Reservations

  • Advance permit required through Yellowstone Backcountry Office
  • Reservation opens: April 1 for current year
  • Maximum stay: 3 nights
  • Fee: $3 per person per night plus $25 permit fee

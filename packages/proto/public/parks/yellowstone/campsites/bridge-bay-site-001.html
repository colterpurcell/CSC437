Bridge Bay Site 001

Up to 6 people RVs up to 40ft Yellowstone Lake shoreline

About This Campsite

Lakefront campsite with direct access to Yellowstone Lake.

Site Features:

  • Lakefront location with direct lake access
  • Fire ring with cooking grate
  • Picnic table
  • Fresh water spigot nearby
  • No hookups
  • Bear box for food storage

Camper Compatibility

Family Campers

Suitable for family camping with amenities and safety features.

  • RV-friendly with level parking
  • Close to restrooms and showers
  • Easy access to visitor services
  • Nearby marina and boat launch

Backpackers

Good base camp for wilderness trips.

  • Resupply access at nearby store
  • Shower facilities available
  • Permit office access for wilderness planning

Bikepackers

Accessible by bike with secure gear storage.

  • Accessible via Grand Loop Road
  • Secure bear box for bike gear
  • Flat area suitable for bike camping setup

Location & Access

How to Get Here

  • From Grand Loop Road: Take Bridge Bay Campground entrance
  • 2.5 miles south of Canyon Junction
  • 0.8 miles from Bridge Bay Marina
  • GPS Coordinates: 44.5234°N, 110.4021°W

Walking Distances

  • Restrooms: 150 yards
  • Showers: 200 yards
  • Camp store: 0.3 miles
  • Marina: 0.8 miles
  • Visitor center: 2.1 miles

Activities

Fishing

  • Direct lake access for cutthroat trout
  • Boat fishing available at marina
  • Wyoming fishing license required

Boating

  • Boat launch at Bridge Bay Marina
  • Rentals available: motorboats, rowboats
  • Guided fishing tours offered

Wildlife Viewing

  • Pelicans and trumpeter swans on lake
  • Bison often graze near campground
  • Black bears frequent the area

Photography

  • Sunrise over lake
  • Mountain reflections in calm water
  • Wildlife photography opportunities

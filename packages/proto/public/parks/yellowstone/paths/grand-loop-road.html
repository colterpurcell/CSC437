Grand Loop Road

Yellowstone's main scenic drive connecting all major attractions

Route Information

  • Distance: 142 miles total
  • Driving time: 4-7 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy - All vehicles

About Grand Loop Road

The Grand Loop Road is Yellowstone's primary scenic drive, forming a figure-eight pattern that connects all the park's major geothermal areas, attractions, and services. This historic road, completed in 1905, provides access to over 95% of Yellowstone's most popular destinations.

Route Highlights:

  • Old Faithful: World's most famous geyser
  • Grand Prismatic Spring: Largest hot spring in the US
  • Lower Falls: Grand Canyon of Yellowstone
  • Hayden Valley: Prime wildlife viewing
  • Mount Washburn: Panoramic mountain views
  • Norris Geyser Basin: Hottest thermal area

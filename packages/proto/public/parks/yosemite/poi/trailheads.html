Yosemite Trailheads

Path starts from Point of Interest: Trailheads providing access to Yosemite's wilderness

Happy Isles Trailhead

Location: Southeast end of Yosemite Valley

Gateway to Mist Trail and waterfall hikes

  • Elevation: 4,035 feet
  • Path leads to: Vernal Fall, Nevada Fall
  • Access: Shuttle stop 16 or walk from parking
  • Popular routes: Mist Trail, John Muir Trail

Yosemite Falls Trailhead

Location: Yosemite Valley near Camp 4

Access to North America's tallest waterfall

  • Path leads to: Lower Falls, Upper Falls
  • Lower Falls: 1 mile round trip (easy)
  • Upper Falls: 7.6 miles round trip (difficult)
  • Parking: Limited roadside parking

Mist Trail Trailhead

Location: Happy Isles area

Famous granite stairway trail to waterfalls

  • Path leads to: Vernal Fall via granite steps
  • Features: 600+ granite steps, waterfall mist
  • Season: Best spring through fall
  • Warning: Steps can be slippery when wet

Mirror Lake Trailhead

Location: End of valley road

Access to seasonal reflecting pool

  • Path leads to: Mirror Lake
  • Distance: 2-5 miles depending on route
  • Difficulty: Easy to moderate
  • Best time: Spring for reflections

Tuolumne Meadows Trailheads

Location: High Country along Tioga Pass Road

Multiple trailheads for backcountry access

  • Elevation: 8,600 feet
  • Path leads to: High Sierra wilderness
  • Camping: Backpacker sites
  • Season: Late June through September

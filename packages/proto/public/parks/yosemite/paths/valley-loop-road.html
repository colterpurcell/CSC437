Yosemite Valley Loop Road

Road is instance of Path. Itinerary travels by Path. Path starts from and ends at Points of Interest.

Route Overview

Location: Yosemite Valley floor

Main scenic drive through Yosemite Valley

  • Total distance: 7.2 mile loop
  • Surface: Paved road
  • Elevation: 4,000 feet (valley floor)
  • Season: Year-round access (weather permitting)

Key Access Points

Path starts from: Valley Entrance

  • Connection: Highway 140, 120, 41
  • Services: Visitor center, parking

Path ends at: Happy Isles Trailhead

  • Shuttle stop 16
  • Access to Vernal and Nevada Falls

Major Stops Along Route

Tunnel View

  • Point of Interest: Classic valley view
  • Features: El Capitan, Bridalveil Fall

Bridalveil Fall Parking

  • Path leads to: Bridalveil Fall
  • Walking distance: 1.2 miles round trip

El Capitan Meadow

  • Features: Views of El Capitan face
  • Activities: Rock climbing viewing

Swinging Bridge

  • Point of Interest: Merced River access
  • Features: River views, swimming access

Mirror Lake Junction

  • Path leads to: Mirror Lake
  • Access: Walking or shuttle

Transportation Options

  • Private vehicle: Allowed with parking restrictions
  • Valley shuttle: Free shuttle system (recommended)
  • Bikepackers: Bike-friendly route
  • Walking: Paved paths alongside road

Camping Access

  • Upper Pines Site 015 - Valley camping
  • Lower Pines Campground - Additional valley sites
  • North Pines Campground - Pet-friendly camping

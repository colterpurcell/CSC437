Tuolumne Meadows Backpacker Site

Camper stays at Campsite. Campsite within Campground. Campground is Point of Interest.

Site Statistics

  • Location: Tuolumne Meadows area
  • Elevation: 8,600 feet
  • Capacity: 6 people maximum
  • Season: June 15 - September 15
  • Site type: Backcountry walk-in

  • Path access: Tioga Pass Road (Highway 120)
  • Distance from road: 0.25 miles walk-in
  • Elevation gain: 100 feet

Facilities

  • Food storage: Bear boxes provided
  • Fire restrictions: Designated rings only
  • Restrooms: Pit toilets

  • Family Campers: Walk-in required

Permits

  • Maximum stay: 7 nights

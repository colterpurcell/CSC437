Upper Pines Site 015

Camper stays at Campsite. Campsite is within Campground. Campground is Point of Interest.

Site Details

Location: Upper Pines Campground, Yosemite Valley

Premium valley floor site with river access

  • Site number: 015
  • Capacity: 6 people, 2 vehicles maximum
  • Elevation: 4,000 feet
  • Season: March through November (weather dependent)

Access

  • Path access: Valley Loop Road
  • Distance from entrance: 6 miles via Valley Loop
  • Parking: On-site parking for 2 vehicles
  • Shuttle access: Shuttle stop 19 (short walk)

Site Features

  • Setting: Merced River access, partial shade
  • Picnic table: Standard campground table
  • Fire ring: Fire restrictions seasonally
  • Water: Spigot within 150 feet
  • Restrooms: Clean facilities nearby
  • Bear box: Food storage required

Nearby Attractions

  • Yosemite Falls - 1 mile walk
  • Bridalveil Fall - 2 miles via shuttle
  • Happy Isles Trailhead - Mist Trail access
  • Merced River - Swimming and fishing
  • Tunnel View - Classic valley photo

Camper Types

  • Family Campers: Ideal site with amenities and space
  • Backpackers: Good base camp for day hikes
  • Bikepackers: Bike-accessible via Valley Loop Road

Reservations

  • Booking opens: 5 months in advance
  • Maximum stay: 7 nights (peak season)
  • Site type: Standard tent/RV site
  • Fee: $36 per night (2024 rates)

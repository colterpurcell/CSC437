How Different Campers Experience This Trip

Family Campers

  • Stay in Upper Pines or Lower Pines Campground
  • Easy valley floor walks and shuttles
  • Visitor center programs and exhibits
  • Short hikes to major viewpoints

Backpackers

  • Backpacking in Tuolumne Meadows area
  • High Sierra camping with permits
  • Alpine lake access and mountain views
  • Challenge hikes like Half Dome cables

Bikepackers

  • Valley Loop Road cycling
  • Bike paths along Merced River
  • Glacier Point Road challenge climb
  • Wawona area exploration

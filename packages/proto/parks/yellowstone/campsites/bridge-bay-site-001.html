Adventure Guide → Parks → Yellowstone → Points of Interest → Campgrounds → Campsites → Bridge Bay Site 001

Activities

Fishing

  • Direct lake access for cutthroat trout
  • Boat fishing available at marina
  • Wyoming fishing license required

Boating

  • Boat launch at Bridge Bay Marina
  • Rentals available: motorboats, rowboats
  • Guided fishing tours offered

Wildlife Viewing

  • Pelicans and trumpeter swans on lake
  • Bison often graze near campground
  • Black bears frequent the area

Photography

  • Sunrise over lake
  • Mountain reflections in calm water
  • Wildlife photography opportunities

Reservations & Rates

Nightly Rates

  • Peak Season (Jun-Aug): $45/night
  • Shoulder Season (May, Sep): $35/night
  • Off Season: $25/night
  • Additional Vehicle: $8/night

How to Reserve

  • Reservations required March-October
  • Book up to 12 months in advance
  • Check-in: 2:00 PM / Check-out: 11:00 AM

Policies

  • Maximum stay: 14 days
  • Quiet hours: 8:00 PM - 8:00 AM
  • Pets allowed on leash
  • Cancellation: 48 hours notice
